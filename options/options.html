About FlowForge

FlowForge

Version 1.0.0

FlowForge is a browser extension that learns your browsing patterns and helps you automate repetitive sequences of navigation.

Key Features:

  • Automatically learns your browsing sequences across websites
  • Suggests automations for frequently repeated sequences
  • One-click execution of saved flows
  • Privacy-focused with local storage and optional encryption

How to Use:

  1. Browse normally - FlowForge will learn your patterns in the background
  2. When a pattern is detected, you'll see a suggestion to save it as a flow
  3. Access your saved flows from the extension popup
  4. Run flows with a single click to automate repetitive browsing tasks

Privacy:

FlowForge respects your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device and never sent to external servers. You can clear your browsing history at any time or enable encryption for additional security.
